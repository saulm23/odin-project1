Mac and Cheese

Description

This homemade mac and cheese is topped with buttered bread crumbs for pure comfort

It's easy to make the cheese sauce from scratch, starting with a roux and adding milk,

Cheeddar and Parmesan to make a rich, decadent sauce that coats every nook and cranny

of the noodles

Skip the boxed stuff and make homemade mac and cheese tonight.

This from-scratch macaroni and cheese recipe will become a taple

in your dinner rotation.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Butter and flour
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Seasoning:Salt and pepper goes into the sauce, while paprika is sprinkled over the topping.
  • Bread Crumbs:Use store-bought dried bread crumbs or make your own at home.

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles, drain, and transfer to a prepared baking dish.
  2. Make the cheese sauce, pour the sauce ove the noodles, and stir.
  3. Make the topping, spread it over macaroni and cheese, and sprinkle with paprika.
  4. Bake the mac and cheese until the topping is golden brown
