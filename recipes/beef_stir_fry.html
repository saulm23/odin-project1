Beef Stir Fry

Description

This classic beef stri-fry is the perfect way to use up some of your leftover

vegetables and other pantry staple ingredients. This beef and broccoli dish comes

together in just 25 minutes and only requires ingredients that you probably already have.

This stir fry is packed with strip of beef steak, veggies, and saucy flavors for

a weeknight dinner warrior that checks all the boxes.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 pound beef sirloin, cut into 2-inch strips
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh brocolli florets
  • 2 carrots, thinly sliced
  • 1 green onion, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons sesame seeds, toasted

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients

  2. Heat vegetable oil in a large wok or skillet over medium-high hear;

    add beef and stir-fry until browned, 3 to 4 minutes.

  3. Move beef to the side of the wok and add broccoli, beli pepper,

    carrots, green onion, and garlic to the center of the wok;

    stir-fry vegetables for 2 minutes.

  4. Stir beef into vegetables and season with soy sauce and sesame seeds.

    Continue to cook and stir until vegetables are tender, about 2 more minutes

  5. Serve hot and enjoy
